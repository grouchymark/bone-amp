Welcome to the BONE Band website!

BONE is a Utah-based band with additional musicians from Wyoming. The band has a 30-plus year history rocking the Wasatch Front and beyond. BONE would likely be classified as primarily a Classic Rock band, but we pride ourselves on performing tunes from an array of genres, including Current Rock, Top-40, Pop, Alternative and Country. Music for All Seasons. Please check out our current overview video (to the right or above/below, depending on the device you're viewing on) and explore the rest of the site. For booking information, please call ‪(801) 918-2806‬ or (307) 288-0070. Thanks for stopping by!

The Players

Preston Kofoed
Bass and Vocals

Preston has been a musician for almost 50 years, is a founding member of BONE and a very accomplished Bass Guitarist and Vocalist. Pres has so many "Brushes with Greatness" stories from his longtime and illustrious music career that are as incredible as they are almost unbelievable!  He's shared musical stages with many big-time names — Stevie Nicks is one that comes to mind... yeah, seriously! Pres also handles the majority of the bookings on behalf of the band. Contact Pres at (801) 918-2806 (Call or Text) or baldmusician@gmail.com

Rett Christensen
Drums and Vocals

Rett was one of the first drummers for BONE way back in the day and returned to the band a few years ago. He is a sensational drummer and vocalist. Rett has shared the stage with a number of big names and has taken part in many productions, both as a musician and producer. When in California, he was employed by an agency that provided rental cars to the rich ad famous, and oh, the stories he can tell! He also makes special appearances wih other bands like "BandAged" Rett can't be with us for all of our gigs, due to other obligations in life, but we love it when can! He's just a class act, who brings so much to  BONE, and a fun guy to boot!

Grady Garrard 
Keyboards and Vocals

Grady is simply a musician's magician. What he brings to BONE with his keyboards and synthesizers is nothing short of incredible, and it rally opens up our songbook. Grady was a part of an earlier iteration of BONE in the early 2000s, replacing founding Member David Stone, when the band reformed. After that, he was an integral part of a highly successful Pink Floyd tribute band, "Floyd Show" and we're fortunate to have him as a part of BONE. Like Rett, Grady can't play all our gigs, especially those away from the Wasatch Front, but it's always a special treat when the "Magician is in the House!"

Mark Madia — Vocals

Mark has only been with the band for the past five or six years, but always had an open invitation to take the stage with BONE to sing a few tunes, since meeting the band back in the mid-80s at the old Whirl Inn in Evanston, where he was the DJ and where BONE began to play often. Mark handles a lot of the band's promotion by producing videos, taking photos and setting up social media presences.

"Big Wave Dave" Wilkinson
Vocals and Bass

Dave is kind of like Mark, in that, he's always been a friend and supporter of the band before he was asked to join. He's an awesome vocalist, whether singing lead or providing harmonies and plays bass, as well. Big Wave has an incredible voval range. He's a fun dude who assists the band in so many ways. Dave plans on expanding his musical talents to help enhance the band's sound.

Doug Worthen
Guitars and Vocals

Doug stepped in during a tough time for BONE, with the passing of our great lead guitarist, James"Cookie" Young, and gave the band the hope we needed to carry on.He's an extremely talented and virsatile guitarist and vocalist,as evidenced by the number of bands he performs with,so we can't have Doug with us as often as we would like, but we'll take him any time we can get him!

Davis Newiger-Walberg
Drums

Davis has become BONE's go-to drummer. He started out with special appearances at BONE gigs and took the time to learn every song listed on a website graphic. Rett can't make all of BONE's gigs, as previously noted. And with Johnni Kennicott joining Jana and the Rebels and Jack Mathson leaving this world way too early, Davis earned his spot with the band. What separates Davis is he's the only member of BONE who does not qualify as an "AARP Rocker." Quite the contrary, as he's in his early 20s, but he's a fantastic drummer BONE is glad to have!

Bruce Holt
Guitars and Vocals 

Bruce is another career musician and an outstanding one at that! He's been the lead guitarist for Jana and the Rebels for a number of years, but after his replacement is found, rumor has it, he will join forces with BONE! His breadth of music, coupled with his creativity and talents will be a most welcome addition for BONE. Bruce also has a solo gig, where he plays at Little America in downtown Salt Lake City every Tuesday evening and is the proud owner of Jitter Bug Coffee Hop — stop in and see him today!

Randall Newiger
Guitars and Vocals

Rand is another longtime musician, originally from California, where he played in numerous bands. He now resides in Evanston, Wyoming, where he owns Solardrive Music and Media, which includes a recording studio on the top floor of the Old Post Office building in beautiful downtown Evanston. Rand has put together and played in a number of Wyoming-based bands, too.Sometimes it's a family affair, as Rand just happens to be Davis' Dad. His bride, Mary, is also musical — Mary sings and plays Bass Guitar, has been in bands and makes guest appearances with BONE, as well.
